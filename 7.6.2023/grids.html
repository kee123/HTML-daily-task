About Mahindra
  • Mahindra & Mahindra Limited is an Indian multinational automotive manufacturing corporation headquartered in Mumbai.
  • It was established in 1945 as Mahindra & Mohammed and later renamed as Mahindra & Mahindra.
  • M&M is one of the largest vehicle manufacturers by production in India.
